About Us

Where Every Pet Is Treated Like Family

Dr. Anil's Pet Animal Clinic has been a trusted name in Andheri East for over 18 years. Our team is dedicated to delivering advanced veterinary care with a warm, compassionate touch.

We understand the deep bond between pets and their families. From routine check-ups to complex surgeries, we provide a safe, stress-free environment so your furry friends feel at ease.
